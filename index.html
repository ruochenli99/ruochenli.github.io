Ruochen Li

I recently completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science at Technical University of Munich (GPA: 1.5/1.0, Distinction), and I am currently seeking PhD opportunities.

My research focuses on Reliable Multimodal Learning and Alignment: evaluation, post-training alignment, and interpretability of LLMs and VLMs.

My previous work bridges language and vision: building clinician-validated benchmarks for radiology report evaluation (X-ray), post-training LLMs (SFT/DPO + LoRA) for fine-grained clinical alignment, rethinking surgical planning evaluation for Video-LLMs, and analyzing attention mechanisms in long-context models. On the vision side, I have worked with MRI and CT/CTA across segmentation, registration, and clinical outcome prediction.

Going forward, I am interested in alignment for multimodal foundation models: deeper vision-language fusion, data-efficient post-training under limited annotation, and LLM/VLM-based agents that reliably follow human intent, task goals, and safety-critical constraints.
